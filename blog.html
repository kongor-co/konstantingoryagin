Unexpected benefit of AI for interpersonal communication

What if learning to talk to AI could help us talk better to each other?

The more I work with AI models, the more I realize something unexpected: Prompting AI isn't just a technical skill — it's a communication masterclass. When you write a good AI prompt, you learn to: Be clear about your expectations Provide context and background Anticipate misunderstandings Remove ambiguity Refine based on feedback Now imagine applying these same techniques to everyday human conversations. Think about how often miscommunication happens because we assume the other person knows what we mean — when in reality, we’re not being specific enough. By practicing how to guide an AI to the outcome we want, we can also learn how to better guide conversations with colleagues, clients, friends — anyone. The bonus? It creates space for empathy. When you focus on clarity and context, you're not just trying to be understood — you're also trying to understand. So maybe learning to prompt AI well is really just training for what we all want more of: deeper human connection. What’s something AI has taught you about real-world communication?
